Chocolate Cake Recipe

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate.

2

Add the sugar, eggs and flour.

3

Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.

4

Bake for 20 min.
